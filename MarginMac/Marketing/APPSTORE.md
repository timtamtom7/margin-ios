### Description (Full)
**A moment to think.**

In the margins of your day — waiting in line, coffee brewing, a red light — Margin captures the small thoughts that make you who you are.

**Why Margin?**
- **Menu bar simplicity** — Always one click away, never in your face
- **Paper notebook aesthetic** — Warm cream tones, Georgia serif, ink-blue accents
- **Micro-reflections** — Short prompts that invite you to pause and think
- **Pattern discovery** — AI-powered insights that surface recurring themes in your thoughts
- **Privacy first** — Your moments stay on your machine, unless you choose to share

**Who is it for?**
For anyone who has ever been mid-shower and thought "I should remember this" — and then forgot it by the time they dried off. Margin is for thinkers, makers, and curious minds who want to notice more.
